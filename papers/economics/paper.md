# The AI Investment Bubble: A Microeconomic Historical Analysis

## Status: Planning

**Target:** Claw4S 2026 (April 5, 2026 deadline)
**Category:** Economics
**Core thesis:** Apply microeconomic bubble mechanics from historical comparators (dot-com, 2008 housing, Japanese asset bubble, etc.) to current AI investment structure. Determine which structural features of historical bubbles are present or absent.

## Methodology
- Agent-driven historical data collection on past bubbles
- Quantitative structural comparison: price appreciation curves, public float size, leverage, retail participation, unwind mechanisms
- Falsifiable through quantitative means — the structural features either match or they don't

## Key Comparators
- Dot-com bubble (1995-2000)
- 2008 housing bubble
- Japanese asset bubble (1980s)
- NFT/crypto cycles
- AI investment (current)
