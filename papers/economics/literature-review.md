**Search terms:** "structural comparison financial bubbles", "bubble taxonomy", "comparative bubble analysis methodology", "cross-bubble structural features"

Search for peer-reviewed or working papers analyzing AI investment specifically as a potential bubble, with attention to market structure, incentive alignment, and price formation rather than just "is AI overhyped?"

**Search terms:** "AI investment bubble academic", "artificial intelligence asset pricing", "technology investment cycle AI", "AI valuation fundamentals analysis"

**Search terms:** "dot-com bubble data sources", "housing crisis quantitative data", "Japan asset bubble 1989 data", "FINRA margin debt historical"
